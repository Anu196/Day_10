This workflow automatically detects the latest job openings from online job APIs, filters relevant jobs, and uses AI to summarize important details such as job title, company, location, skills, and application link. The results can be sent by email or stored for future reference.


## **AI Job Detection & Job Summary (Day-10)**

### **Project Description**

The **AI Job Detection & Job Summary** project is an **n8n automation workflow** that automatically retrieves the latest job openings from online job APIs. It filters relevant job listings based on user-defined criteria and uses AI to generate concise summaries, including the job title, company, location, required skills, and application link. The processed results can be sent via email or stored in a database or Google Sheets for future reference.

---

## **Features**

* 💼 Automatically fetches the latest job openings using Job APIs
* 🔍 Filters jobs based on relevant criteria
* 🤖 AI-generated summaries of job details
* 🏢 Displays job title, company, location, required skills, and application link
* 📧 Sends job updates automatically via email

* 🔄 Fully automated workflow built with n8n
